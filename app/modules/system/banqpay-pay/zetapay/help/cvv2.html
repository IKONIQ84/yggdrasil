Card verification number
For safe and secure credit card transactions, BanQpay requires you to enter your card's verification number. The verification number is a 3-digit number printed on the back of your credit card. It appears after your card number toward the right of your card.
[American Express card]
American Express cards have the verification number printed on the front of your card. It is a 4-digit number that appears on the right shoulder of your card number.
