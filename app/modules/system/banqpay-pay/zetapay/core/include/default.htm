BanqPay is a new online payment service, that allows visitors to pay for services on auctions, classified sites, and personal web sites.

In contrast with other online payment services, we are focused on enabling payments using dynamic generated content and payments enabling the dynamic link on a content provider website. Deploying this method allows the content provider to control the access to a specific link, by time or/and number of visits.

We have concluded our Alpha testing and as part of this project stage we studied actual performances, and combine various payments methods to deliver the final merchant service to content providers.

We are moving into the Beta testing phase. We are currently looking for 10 content merchants to participate in the Beta testing phase, if you are interested please contact beta@epnn.com

The BanQpay project was commissioned by the ePaymentsnews Network (www.epnn.com), to handle content payment, subscriptions and purchase payments under one payment service.

If you are interested in seeing how you can make a purchase using a link on your website, visit ePaymentsnews Network and click on a pay as you go link.
